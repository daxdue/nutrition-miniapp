Privacy Policy

Last updated: 07 Dec 2025

About this Service

Nutrition AI Assistant Bot (“the Bot”, “the Service”) analyzes meal photos, estimates nutrition, and provides basic dietary tracking.

This privacy policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

  • Telegram User ID, username, and public profile data
  • Meal photos and captions you send
  • AI-generated nutrition analysis (calories, macros, meal type)
  • Timestamps and technical metadata

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Analyze meal photos via OpenAI
  • Store your nutrition history
  • Display statistics inside the Telegram WebApp
  • Improve service accuracy and stability

3. Third-Party Processing (OpenAI)

Photos you send are uploaded to OpenAI for recognition. OpenAI processes images according to their guidelines:

https://openai.com/policies

4. Legal Basis

We rely on:

  • Performance of a service
  • Legitimate interest
  • Consent by action (sending photos)

5. Data Retention

We store your data until you request deletion or stop using the service.

6. Your Rights

You may request:

  • Access to your data
  • Correction of data
  • Complete deletion

7. Data Deletion

Use the /delete command inside the bot to permanently delete your data.

8. Security

We use industry-standard security practices, but no system is perfectly secure.
